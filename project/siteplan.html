Site purpose: Our Goal

Share ideas and tools on how to store food for a year supply, and also other resources like videos and web pages related to food storage.

We are accustomed to simply going to the store whenever we need food.
But what if one day we can't find the store open?
That's why we need to be prepared for emergencies like that, such as
stocking up on essential supplies or learning how to grow our own food.

Food storage is a way of decreasing the variability of the food supply in the
face of natural, inevitable variability. It allows food to be eaten for some time
(typically weeks to months) after harvest rather than solely immediately.

Scenarios: Our view

Why do I need a food storage supply?

  • The purpose is to provide for our needs and be prepare for every needful thing in case of an emergency.
  • To be prepare for adversity in life by having a basic supply of food and water and some money in savings.
