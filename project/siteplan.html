Site Purpose

In this page I will share ideas and tools on how to store food for a year supply, and also other resources like videos and webpages related to store food.

Escenarios

Why do I need a food storage supply?

  • The purpose is to provide for our needs and be prepare for every needful thing in case of an emergency.
  • To be prepare for adversity in life by having a basic supply of food and water and some money in savings.

What Am I supposed to have in my food storage?

  • Food Supply (Three-month and long-term)
  • Water Supply
  • Financial reserve
