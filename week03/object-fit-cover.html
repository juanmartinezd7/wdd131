object-fit: cover

Containers: 200 x 200 | 1:1

Og. Images: 700 x 300 | 7:3

CSS: width and height 100%; distorted

CSS: with object-fit: cover;

This property forces the image to cover the entire container while maintaining its aspect ratio. This often involves scaling the image, either up or down. If an image has a different aspect ratio than the container (e.g., a square image in a rectangular container), object-fit: cover; will crop parts of the image to make it fit. This cropping can sometimes lead to awkward compositions or cut-off important details.
